# 📈 TradeTwin AI

> **Your AI-Powered Trading Copilot for Smarter, Safer & More Disciplined Investing**

TradeTwin AI is an intelligent trading assistant designed to help retail traders make informed decisions using Artificial Intelligence, technical analysis, market sentiment, portfolio insights, and personalized risk management.

Instead of simply displaying market data, TradeTwin AI explains **why** a stock is moving, identifies potential opportunities, evaluates risks, and helps users avoid emotional trading mistakes.

## 📖 Problem Statement

Retail traders often lose money not because they lack information, but because they struggle to make fast, informed, and emotionally disciplined trading decisions.

Most trading platforms provide charts and indicators but fail to:

* Explain market movements in simple language
* Detect emotional trading behaviors such as FOMO and panic selling
* Provide personalized risk management strategies
* Combine technical analysis, news sentiment, and AI guidance
* Help beginners learn while actively trading

As a result, traders frequently:

* Enter trades too early or too late
* Exit profitable positions prematurely
* Hold losing positions for too long
* Take excessive risks
* Experience information overload

---

## 💡 Solution

TradeTwin AI acts as an intelligent digital trading twin that analyzes:

* Live market data
* Technical indicators
* Market sentiment
* Portfolio exposure
* Risk metrics
* Trading behavior patterns

The platform delivers personalized, explainable trading insights before, during, and after every trade, helping users trade with confidence rather than emotion.
