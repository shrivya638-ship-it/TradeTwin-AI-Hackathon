About

Retail traders lose money not because information is unavailable, but because they struggle to analyze vast amounts of market data, understand why stocks move, manage risk, and control emotional decision-making. Existing trading platforms provide charts and numbers but fail to offer simple explanations, AI-driven insights, personalized guidance, and risk analysis in one place. As a result, beginners feel overwhelmed, and even experienced traders can make impulsive decisions that lead to losses
